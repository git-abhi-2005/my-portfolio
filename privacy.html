Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me. This policy explains how I collect, use, and protect any information you share when you visit or interact with this portfolio website.

01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal portfolio website of Abhishek. By visiting this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. I am committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how it is handled.

This website does not sell or rent your personal data to any third parties. Any information collected is used solely to improve your experience on this site.

02

Information We Collect

I may collect limited information when you interact with this portfolio, primarily to understand how visitors use the site and to respond to any contact requests.

Personal Information

When you reach out through the contact form, I may collect the following details:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message or inquiry
  • Any other details you voluntarily provide

Automated Data Collection

Like most websites, some data may be collected automatically when you visit, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
03

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected through this website is used only for the following purposes:

Responding to your contact or project inquiries
Improving the content and user experience of this site
Analyzing website traffic and visitor behavior
Ensuring the security and performance of the website
Complying with any applicable legal obligations
Sending a reply or follow-up regarding your message
04

Information Sharing and Disclosure

I do not share your personal information with third parties except in the limited circumstances described below:

With Your Consent

Your information may be shared with third parties only if you have given explicit consent to do so, such as when collaborating on a project or referring you to another service provider.

Legal Requirements

I may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, including:

  • Compliance with a court order or legal process
  • Protecting the rights and safety of this website
  • Investigating potential violations of this policy
  • Responding to lawful requests by public authorities
05

Data Security

I take reasonable steps to protect any information collected through this website from unauthorized access or misuse:

Encryption

This website uses HTTPS to ensure all data transmitted between your browser and the server is encrypted.

Access Control

Access to any collected data is strictly limited and not shared with unauthorized individuals.

Data Protection

Personal data submitted via contact forms is stored securely and deleted once no longer needed.

06

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data collected through this website:

Access Your Data

You can request a copy of any personal data I hold about you at any time.

Correct Information

If any information I hold is inaccurate, you can request that it be corrected.

Delete Your Data

You may request deletion of your personal data, and I will comply where legally permitted.

Object to Processing

You have the right to object to how your data is being used in certain circumstances.

07

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in how this website operates or to comply with legal requirements. Any updates will be reflected with a revised date at the top of this page.

I encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how your information is being protected.
